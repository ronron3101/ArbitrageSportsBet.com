How to evaluate an alternative

  1. Confirm your country and bookmaker coverage first.
  2. Check whether the tool focuses on sure bets, value bets, middles, props, promo conversion, or a mix.
  3. Compare total monthly price against realistic bankroll and expected edge.
  4. Look for trial access before committing annually.
  5. Understand account limitation risk before scaling stakes.
