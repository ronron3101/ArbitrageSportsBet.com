Australia-first buying criteria

  1. Bookmaker coverage: confirm Australian bookmaker support before paying.
  2. Exchange support: check whether exchange availability matters for your strategy.
  3. Odds freshness: delayed odds can make small arbitrage margins unusable.
  4. Account limitation risk: arbing can lead to stake restrictions or account closure.
  5. Bankroll fit: subscription price must be sensible relative to your stake size and expected edge.
